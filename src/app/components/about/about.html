How It Works

Slim Reviews is a lightweight, embeddable review system designed to help creators, small businesses, and projects collect meaningful feedback with minimal friction. Whether you're managing one site or many, Slim Reviews adapts seamlessly to your needs.

  • Embed Instantly: Add a single JavaScript snippet to your site—no complex setup needed.
  • Flexible Project Setup: Each review widget can represent a different page, section, or campaign within your domain.
  • Privacy First: Users can leave anonymous reviews—no registration required.
  • Simple Dashboard: Easily view, moderate, and manage reviews through a clean admin portal.

Whether you're gathering testimonials, feedback, or lightweight comments, Slim Reviews keeps things fast, secure, and user-focused.
