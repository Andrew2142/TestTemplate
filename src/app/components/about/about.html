How It Works

Slim Reviews is a lightweight, embeddable review system designed to help creators, small businesses, and projects collect meaningful feedback with minimal friction. Whether you're managing one site or many, Slim Reviews adapts seamlessly to your needs.

  • Embed Instantly: Drop a single JavaScript snippet into your site to start collecting reviews immediately no complex setup required.
  • Project-Based Organization: Each site or domain can be linked to its own project, giving you granular control over where reviews go.
  • Privacy First: Users can leave reviews anonymously no registration required.
  • Simple Dashboard: View, moderate, and manage your widget from a clean, user-friendly admin portal.

Whether you're gathering testimonials, feedback, or lightweight comments, Slim Reviews keeps things fast, secure, and user-focused.
