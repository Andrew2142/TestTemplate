How It Works

Slim Reviews is a lightweight, embeddable review system for creators, small businesses, and projects — helping you collect meaningful feedback with minimal friction.

Embed Instantly

Add a single JavaScript snippet to your site—no complex setup needed.

Flexible Project Setup

Each review widget can represent a different page, section, or campaign within your domain.

Privacy First

Users can leave anonymous reviews—no registration required.

Simple Dashboard

Easily view, moderate, and manage reviews through a clean admin portal.

Whether you're gathering testimonials, feedback, or lightweight comments, Slim Reviews keeps things fast, secure, and user-focused.
